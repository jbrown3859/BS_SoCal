Pele

Pele is our rounded pin tail model featuring a single concave starting in the first sixteen inches and extending all the way to the tail. The rails are relatively knifey but have enough volume to keep you in the wave; with a hard edge starting in the last 18 inches. It works well in surf from 2-3ft and handles well into about a foot overhead. It features extra volume under the chest for simplified paddling in order to help make some of those faster waves. Works well in beach breaks, and on point breaks.
