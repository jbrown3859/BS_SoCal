Sword

The sword got its name from being relatively flat both in entry rocker and exit rocker. Featuring a somewhat deep single concave, a flat plank like tail, and an extremely hard edge the board can really open up and let loose. It has an extremely flat deck, and very little rocker, but enough paddle ability to make almost all sections. The sword is able to handle surf from 3-4 ft and up to 2 feet overhead. Works well with faster waves and big turnable sections. Great for point breaks
