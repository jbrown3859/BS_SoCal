RWB

RWB is a high performance squash tail that will rip through some nasty surf that's thrown at it. Handling everything from small 3 foot junk up to 2 foot overhead. It features a little bit of extra rocker in the nose it makes for taking drops a little bit easier. There is enough under the chest though as to not lose paddle ability. The single concave that runs all the way through the back of board, allows for extra hold in the steeper sections, while giving it looseness out the tail to make it so it can still turn with ease. The RWB is a high performance board that can really rip and will be a nice addition in any quiver.
