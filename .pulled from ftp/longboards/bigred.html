Big Red

Big Red is your old school nose rider. If you want to hang your toes up off the nose, and sit there then this board is for you. It has a single concave in the nose to aid with lift, and to hold you on the face of the wave. Theres slight rocker out the back but relatively flat wide square tail. It has sharp rails that can dig into the face of the wave, allowing the board to hold without sinking. It gives the good glide feeling that you are just floating on top of the water. In the tail it's about 14 inches wide, and about 18 in the nose.
